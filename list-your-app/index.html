How It Works
How It Works

List once, sell forever

Get your app in front of sellers who are actively looking for apps to pitch to businesses.

Submit your app

Send us your app details — what it does, who it's for, screenshots, and your floor price (what you need to be paid).

We list & promote it

We add 10% to your floor price to set the listed price. Our sellers see it and start pitching it to businesses in their network.

Build, get paid

When a seller closes a deal, you build the app for the client. You receive your full floor price plus 50% of everything sold above the listed price.

Revenue Model

Transparent revenue split

Here's how the money flows when a seller closes a deal for your app.

Example: Your floor is $3,000 · Listed at $3,300 · Sold for $5,000

You receive
$3,850
Seller profit (50%)
$850
Platform fee (10%)
$300

You set your floor price — what you need to get paid. We add 10% on top to create the listed floor price ($3,000 → $3,300). That 10% ($300) is our platform fee. Sellers must sell at least $500 above the listed price. Everything above the listed price ($5,000 − $3,300 = $1,700) is split 50/50 between you and the seller — so you get $3,000 + $850 = $3,850, and the seller gets $850.
