Digital check-in & check-out

Guests can check in before they even arrive — upload their ID, sign the registration form, and receive their digital room key. When it's time to leave, one tap settles the bill and completes check-out. No queues at reception, no waiting, no paperwork. Hotels save staff time and guests get a frictionless arrival experience.

  • Pre-arrival digital check-in
  • ID upload and verification
  • Digital room key via QR code
  • One-tap express check-out
  • Automated billing summary

Room services & requests

Everything a guest might need — right from their phone. Order room service from the restaurant menu, request extra towels, set a do-not-disturb status, ask for room cleaning, or report a maintenance issue. Each request goes straight to the right department with no miscommunication. Guests don't have to call anyone, and staff get organized, trackable requests instead of scattered phone calls.

  • In-room dining and room service ordering
  • Do Not Disturb toggle
  • Request room cleaning on demand
  • Report problems (maintenance, noise, etc.)
  • Real-time request status tracking
  • Direct routing to the right department

Hotel services, FAQ & bookings

Guests can explore everything the hotel offers — spa treatments, massages, pool access, gym sessions, guided tours, airport transfers, and any other service the hotel provides. A built-in FAQ section answers the most common questions (Wi-Fi password, breakfast hours, late check-out policy) so guests get instant answers without calling reception. For everything else, there's a direct chat with the front desk.

  • Browse and book spa, massage, and wellness services
  • Schedule any hotel service (tours, transfers, etc.)
  • Comprehensive FAQ section
  • Wi-Fi info, breakfast hours, pool times at a glance
  • Direct chat with front desk
  • Push notifications for booking confirmations
