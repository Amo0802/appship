The guest experience, reimagined

A complete digital concierge for hotels, resorts, and boutique stays. Digital check-in and check-out, room services, spa and massage booking with full pricing, resort mini map, hotel events, problem reporting, and a guest FAQ — everything from the guest's phone. No more calls to the front desk.

Screen 1

Digital check-in and check-out

Guests check in before they arrive — upload ID, sign forms digitally, and receive a digital room key. Check-out is a single tap: the bill is settled, the room is released, and a receipt is sent automatically. No queues at reception, no paperwork, no waiting.

  • Pre-arrival digital check-in
  • ID upload and verification
  • Digital room key via QR code
  • One-tap express check-out
  • Automated billing summary
Screen 2

Room services and requests

Everything a guest might need — from their phone. Order room service from the restaurant menu, toggle Do Not Disturb, request room cleaning, or report a maintenance issue. Each request is routed directly to the right department with full tracking. Guests never need to pick up the phone.

  • In-room dining and room service
  • Do Not Disturb toggle
  • Request room cleaning on demand
  • Report problems — maintenance, noise, etc.
  • Real-time request status tracking
  • Direct routing to the right department
Screen 3

Services, events, and resort map

Guests browse and book hotel services — spa treatments, massages, sauna sessions, airport transfers — each with full descriptions, pricing, and available time slots. A mini map helps guests navigate the resort. Upcoming hotel events are shown on a calendar. The built-in FAQ answers common questions like Wi-Fi passwords and breakfast hours instantly.

  • Spa, massage, and sauna booking with pricing
  • Service details — descriptions, times, availability
  • Interactive mini map of the resort
  • Hotel events calendar
  • Comprehensive guest FAQ
  • Push notifications for booking confirmations
