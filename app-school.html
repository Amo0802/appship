Connect the whole school

A multi-role platform for administrators, teachers, students, and parents. Schedules, grades, QR attendance, class materials, test notifications, parent meetings, and more — everything a modern school needs in one branded app.

Screen 1

Multi-role dashboard

Each user sees a personalized home screen based on their role. Students see upcoming classes, pending assignments, and recent grades. Teachers manage their schedule, input grades, and organize materials. Parents monitor their child's performance, attendance, and upcoming meetings. Admins oversee the entire system.

  • Role-based access — admin, teacher, student, parent
  • Personalized dashboard per role
  • Quick access to schedule and grades
  • Notification center for announcements
  • School events and calendar
Screen 2

Schedules, materials, and attendance

Full class timetable for students and teachers with real-time updates for cancellations or room changes. Teachers upload class materials — PDFs, presentations, links — that students access directly in the app. Attendance is tracked via QR code: students scan at the start of each class, and absences are logged automatically.

  • Interactive class timetable
  • Class material uploads and downloads
  • QR code attendance scanning
  • Automatic absence logging
  • Schedule change notifications
Screen 3

Grades, tests, and parent portal

Teachers input grades, test scores, and written feedback per student. Students see their results in real time with averages calculated per subject. Parents get a dedicated view showing their child's grades, attendance record, upcoming tests, scheduled meetings, and any disciplinary notes or warnings. Push notifications keep parents informed without needing to check the app constantly.

  • Grade and test score management
  • Subject averages and performance trends
  • Test scheduling with student notifications
  • Parent view — grades, absences, warnings
  • Teacher-parent meeting scheduling
  • Disciplinary notes and penalty tracking
