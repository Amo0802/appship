Every member's fitness hub

Give gym members a premium digital experience. Membership management, QR check-in, training session schedules, live occupancy, workout tracking, and detailed program info — all in one branded app. Perfect for gyms, studios, and fitness centers.

Screen 1

Membership and QR check-in

Members see their active membership plan, renewal date, and payment status at a glance. QR code check-in replaces physical key cards — members scan at the door and they're in. The gym can track who's in the building and when, with full attendance history.

  • Digital membership card with plan details
  • QR code gym check-in
  • Membership tier management
  • Attendance history
  • In-app membership payments
Screen 2

Sessions, programs, and live occupancy

Members browse all available training sessions — group classes like pilates and HIIT, individual personal training, and open gym slots. Each program shows its schedule, trainer, description, and available spots. The live occupancy feature shows how many people are currently in the gym so members can plan their visit.

  • Group and individual session schedules
  • Program details — pilates, HIIT, personal training
  • Real-time gym occupancy counter
  • Trainer profiles and availability
  • Session booking and waitlists
Screen 3

Workout tracking and progress

Members log their workouts, track gym days, and monitor progress over time. Stats show total visits, streak days, workout frequency, and time spent per session. It's the kind of data that keeps members motivated and coming back — and gives the gym proof their app delivers real value.

  • Workout session logging
  • Visit count and streak tracking
  • Time spent per session
  • Progress charts over time
  • Personal records and milestones
