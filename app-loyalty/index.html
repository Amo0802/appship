Turn visitors into regulars

A QR-based points and rewards program that keeps customers coming back. Customers scan their receipt to earn points and redeem them for rewards — simple, engaging, and effective for any retail or hospitality business.

Platform

Screen 1

Points dashboard and QR scanning

Customers see their current points balance, recent transactions, and how close they are to their next reward — all on a clean home screen. To earn points, they simply scan the QR code on their receipt after a purchase. Points are credited instantly with a confirmation animation that makes the experience feel rewarding.

  • Current points balance at a glance
  • QR code receipt scanning to earn points
  • Instant point crediting with confirmation
  • Transaction history log
  • Progress toward next reward tier
Screen 2

Rewards catalog and redemption

A browsable catalog of available rewards — free items, discounts, exclusive offers, and limited-time perks. Each reward shows how many points it costs, and customers can redeem with a single tap. The business controls what's available and can rotate rewards to keep things fresh and drive repeat visits.

  • Browsable rewards catalog
  • One-tap redemption
  • Points cost per reward clearly shown
  • Limited-time and exclusive offers
  • Redemption history
Screen 3

Promotions and business insights

The business sends targeted push notifications for double-point days, flash rewards, and seasonal promotions. On the backend, they see analytics on customer visit frequency, spending patterns, reward redemption rates, and program engagement — actionable data that helps them understand and grow their customer base.

  • Push notifications for promotions
  • Double-point events and flash rewards
  • Customer visit and spending analytics
  • Redemption rate tracking
  • Segmented customer campaigns
