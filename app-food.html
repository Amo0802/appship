Order ahead, get it delivered

Give restaurants, cafés, and fast food chains a complete mobile ordering solution. Customers browse the full menu, order for delivery, dine-in, or takeaway, pay online, and track their order in real time — all from their phone.

Screen 1

Full digital menu with categories

The entire restaurant menu organized by categories — appetizers, mains, drinks, desserts, combos. Each item shows photos, descriptions, pricing, and available modifiers like size, extras, and special requests. Customers can quickly find what they want with search and filters.

  • Organized categories and subcategories
  • Item photos, descriptions, and pricing
  • Modifiers and add-ons per item
  • Search and dietary filters
  • Special requests field
Screen 2

Delivery, dine-in, and takeaway

Customers choose how they want their food — delivered to their door, ready for pickup, or served at their table. Each mode has its own flow: delivery shows estimated times and address management, takeaway shows pickup time slots, and dine-in lets customers order directly from their table via QR code.

  • Delivery with real-time tracking
  • Takeaway with pickup time selection
  • Dine-in table ordering via QR
  • Saved delivery addresses
  • Estimated preparation time
Screen 3

Online payments and order tracking

Secure in-app payments with Apple Pay, Google Pay, and card payments. Once an order is placed, customers see live status updates — confirmed, preparing, ready, on the way — with push notifications at every stage. Order history keeps a record of past orders for easy reordering.

  • Apple Pay, Google Pay, and card payments
  • Real-time order status updates
  • Push notifications at each stage
  • Order history and quick reorder
  • Promo codes and discounts
