Revenue Model

Transparent revenue split

Here's how the money flows when a seller closes a deal for your app.

Example: Your app sold for $5,000 (floor: $3,000)

You receive (floor)
$3,000
Seller profit (45%)
$900
Platform fee (10%)
$200

You set the floor price — what you need to get paid. Sellers must sell at least $500 above it. The $2,000 profit above the floor is split three ways: 45% to the seller ($900), 45% to you ($900), and 10% to Appshipping ($200) as a platform fee.
