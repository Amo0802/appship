Screen 1

Product catalog and discovery

Customers browse the full product catalog organized by categories, collections, and filters. Featured products, new arrivals, and sale items get highlighted on the home screen. Search with auto-suggestions helps customers find exactly what they're looking for, while sorting by price, popularity, and rating makes browsing effortless.

  • Categories, collections, and filters
  • Featured products and promotions
  • Search with auto-suggestions
  • Sort by price, popularity, and rating
  • Wishlist and recently viewed
Screen 2

Product details and variants

Each product page shows high-resolution image galleries, detailed descriptions, sizing information, and customer reviews. Variants like size, color, and material are easy to select. Stock availability is shown in real time, and related product suggestions encourage additional purchases.

  • High-res image galleries with zoom
  • Size, color, and variant selection
  • Stock availability indicators
  • Customer reviews and ratings
  • Related product recommendations
Screen 3

Checkout and order tracking

A frictionless checkout flow with saved addresses, multiple payment methods, and discount code support. After purchase, customers track their order from processing through shipping to delivery with real-time status updates and push notifications. Order history makes repeat purchases quick and easy.

  • Apple Pay, Google Pay, and card payments
  • Saved addresses and payment methods
  • Discount and promo code support
  • Real-time shipment tracking
  • Order history and easy reordering
