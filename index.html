How It Works

Three steps. Zero risk.

Appshipping is like dropshipping — but for mobile apps. You find the client, close the deal, and we build & deliver the app.

01

Find a client

Restaurants, gyms, schools, online stores — thousands of businesses need an app but don't know where to start. You connect them with us.

02

Sell above the floor price

Each app has a minimum price. You must sell at least $500 above the floor — that guarantees you earn a minimum of $250 per deal.

03

We handle the rest

Design, development, testing, App Store submission, hosting, support — we do all the work. You get paid as the client pays.

The Rules

How deals & payments work

Clear rules, no surprises. Here's what you need to know before you start selling.

Contract first, then sell

Before you sell a single app, we sign a partnership agreement together. This protects both sides — your commissions, our standards, and the client's expectations.

Minimum $500 above floor

Every app must be sold for at least $500 above the floor price. This guarantees you earn a minimum of $250 per deal — we don't want you working for less.

Get paid immediately

Clients pay 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. You receive your share of each payment as it comes in — so you earn money the moment you close the deal.

50/50 above the floor

Everything the client pays above the floor price is split equally between you and us. Sell higher, earn more — there's no cap on your earnings.

Appshipping

  • ✓$0 upfront cost

  • ✓No coding needed

FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Do I need technical skills? +

Not at all. We handle all design, development, testing, and deployment. Your job is finding clients and closing deals.

How do I get paid? +

Clients pay 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. You get your 50% share of the profit from each payment as it comes in — so you earn the moment you close the deal.

Is there a contract? +

Yes. We sign a partnership agreement before you start selling. It covers pricing, payment terms, and responsibilities — protecting both sides.

Can I sell to anyone, anywhere? +

Yes. No geographic restrictions. Any business that needs a mobile app is a potential client.

What's the minimum I can earn per deal? +

Since you must sell at least $500 above the floor and the split is 50/50, your minimum is $250 per deal. Most sellers earn significantly more.

How long does delivery take? +

Most apps are built, tested, and live on both App Store and Google Play within 30 days of the client approving the design.

What if the client wants custom features? +

Send us the requirements, we'll adjust the floor price accordingly, and you sell at your margin.

What about ongoing support? +

We offer hosting and maintenance plans to clients after launch. This is handled and priced separately — you can offer it as an upsell.
